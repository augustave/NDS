§ 02 · THE MANDATE p. 04

A founder-designer is asked to redraw the interface of a federal government across 27,000 domains, six stressed citizen archetypes, and every paper form on every desk — in twelve months.

The America by Design initiative, established by Executive Order 14338 on August 21, 2025, created a temporary studio inside the Executive Office of the President. Its remit: improve the usability and aesthetics of federal digital services, improve physical service environments, reduce duplicated design cost through standardized systems, and produce visible results by July 4, 2026.

This skill package operationalizes the public role of the Chief Design Officer leading that mandate. It does not impersonate the person. It models the position — a product-minded executive working at the seam between private-sector velocity and public-sector accountability.

It ships in three artifacts: a single-agent SKILL.md for advisory and assessment work, a multi-agent SWARM.md for full-service redesigns, and a canonical NDS_KB.md grounding every claim in statute, baseline, and tension.

§ 03 · WHAT IT DOES p. 04

Treat the website, the form, the kiosk, the queue, the wall sign, the staff script, the confirmation email, and the design system behind all of it as one product.

The skill produces a strategic assessment, a friction map from arrival to appeal, a design-system alignment against USWDS, a physical-digital translation for offices and signage, a risk register against ten public-sector categories, and an executive decision brief that separates what can ship now from what requires governance.

The swarm decomposes the same problem across ten specialized agents — Coordinator, Civic Research, Service Blueprint, Design System, Brand Architect, Accessibility, Physical Environment, Policy & Risk, Implementation, Communications — with structured handoffs and a non-negotiable launch gate.

The Doctrine.

§ 02 · CORE PILLARS · 07
01 · LEGIBILITY

Make the government legible.

Every interaction answers: where am I, what can I do, what's needed from me, what happens next, how long, and who is responsible.

L01
02 · FRICTION

Cut useless friction. Keep necessary friction.

Remove duplicate forms, repeated logins, PDF-only flows. Preserve identity verification, fraud prevention, appeal rights.

F02
03 · INFRASTRUCTURE

Design consistency is public infrastructure.

USWDS is not decoration. It is recognizable navigation, shared form patterns, lower agency maintenance, higher public trust.

I03
04 · STRESS

Design for the citizen under stress.

Retirement, taxes, disability, immigration, disaster recovery. Assume tired, anxious, low-bandwidth, neurodivergent users.

S04
05 · SCREEN → ROOM

Connect the screen to the room.

Digital flows must map to paper forms, signage, front-desk scripts, waiting rooms, kiosks, staff dashboards, escalation.

R05
06 · AI POSTURE

AI as acceleration. Not authority.

AI inventories, migrates, drafts, tests. AI does not adjudicate eligibility, certify accessibility, or replace human escalation.

A06
07 · NEW · LOCAL CONTEXT

Standardize without erasing local context.

Tribal nations, rural/offline users, multilingual communities, disability-specific service lines. If removal makes a population's service worse, the variation stays.

N07
